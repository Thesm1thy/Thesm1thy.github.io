More About Me

I graduated from the University of New Haven in 2023 with my Bachelors degree in computer science. While studying there I also competed in Division II sports running Track & Field as well as a season of Cross-Country. Durring my senior year for my capstone project I dreamed up the idea to create a VR game that can be controlled with your mind, and was able to come together with my partners and turn this idea into a REALITY. We were also fortunate to be able to present our project at CCSCNE and GameDev CT. One of my favorite hobbies to do/watch is sports like weightlifting, snowboarding, and basketball. Then when I have free time I usually spend it playing video games alone or online with friends.

My career goal is to design and create software that users can enjoy and utilize regularly, wether that be a website, an application, or even a game. I am a great team player and love collaborating with teammates but I am also willing to take the lead on a project if neccessary. I plan on creating my own projects for my hobbies like an application that will suggest songs/albums to listen to based on multiple factors and then connect to your playlist to save/listen to the songs.
